Sentence Slice Prototype

Swipe across the sentence strip. The prototype only cuts at legal spaces between words, snaps the cut to the nearest valid boundary, and prevents splitting a word in half.

How this prototype behaves

  • Swipe through words to split only at legal spaces between words.
  • Drag the outer lane around a row horizontally to reveal clipped text.
  • Drag the bag from the right tool belt onto a piece to wrap it as a clause row.
  • Wrapped rows can be split further and all resulting pieces stay inside that wrap.
