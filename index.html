How this prototype behaves

  • Swipe to split only at legal spaces between words.
  • Drag label tools from the right belt onto sliced elements to color-tag them.
  • Glue tool is toggle-based: click tool, pick element A, pick element B to connect them.
  • If an answer key exists for this sentence, labels are auto-checked on drop.
